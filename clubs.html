Delta Sigma Pi (DSP)

Website Instagram

Delta Sigma Pi is a co-ed professional business fraternity organized to foster the study of business in universities and was founded on November 7, 1907 at New York University by Harold Valentine Jacobs, Alexander Frank Makay, Alfred Moysello, and Henry Albert Tienken. With over 309,000 members and 200 collegiate chapters around the country, Delta Sigma Pi continues to develop principled business leaders for the future by providing a lifetime of opportunity for our members.
